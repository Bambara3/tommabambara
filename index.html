My Story

I am from the Land of Upright Men, also knowned as Burkina Faso, a small country located in West Africa. Since my childhood, I always had the strong desire, the ambition to explore the West so "Work harder than the day before" quickly became my mantra. All the efforts and time fortunately paid. In 2016, I was awarded a full scholarship to continue my High School in British Columbia, Canada.

There I had the amazing opportunity to interact with peers coming from all around the world and successfully achieved my International Baccalaureate in the span of two years. Once again based on academic achievement, I was awarded another full scholarship to continute my undergraduate in the University of Oklahoma, prestigious for its college of Business.

It is no wonder then I am pursuing an Accelerated Master's Program in Management Information Systems and a Bachelor in Applied Economics and I envisage to finish both my degrees in May 2022.
